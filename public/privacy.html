Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Information: When you authenticate with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information.

API Keys: We generate and store secure API keys associated with your account.

Usage Data: We collect basic usage statistics and error logs to improve service reliability.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not read, store, or access the content of your Google Sheets
  • We do not store your Google authentication tokens permanently
  • We do not share your data with third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and provide API access
  • To generate and manage your secure API keys
  • To facilitate communication between your applications and Google Sheets
  • To provide customer support when requested

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • JWT tokens with expiration for session management
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by revoking access.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Google Sheets API. Your use of Google services is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request information about data we store
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Your data remains accessible through standard Google Sheets exports

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy via email or service notifications.
